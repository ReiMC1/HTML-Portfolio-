My Top Three Movie Choices

By Reinaldo Morales Cruz

"Her" by Spike Jonez

A touching movie about love, loneliness, and artificial intellegince.

"Forrest Gump" by Robert Zemeckis

When chance, opportunity, and resiliance are in the hands of someone who only sees the good in everything.

"Fantastic Mr. Fox" by Wes Anderson

This movie is about being grateful for what you have.
